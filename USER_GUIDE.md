## üöÄ Getting Started

### 1. Account Creation (Sign Up)
To begin, visit the landing page and click on **Sign Up**.
*   Enter your **Full Name**.
*   Enter a valid **Email Address**.
*   Create a secure **Password**.

> [!TIP]
> Use your work email if you plan to join or create a company organization.

---

### 2. Choosing Your Path (Personal vs. Enterprise)
After signing up, you will be prompted to choose how you want to use OpsMind AI. This is a critical step!

#### **Option A: Personal Use (Individual)**
*   **Best for**: Freelancers, researchers, or individuals who want to manage their own private PDFs.
*   **Experience**: Your workspace is private to just you. 
*   **Pricing**: Starts with a free tier.

#### **Option B: Enterprise Use (Team/Company)**
*   **Best for**: Companies, HR departments, or teams.
*   **Experience**: Allows you to create an Organization. You can invite team members (Employees/Managers) and share SOPs across the whole company.
*   **Pricing**: Enjoy a **Free Trial** for your organization (limited to 3 users and 3 documents).

---

### 3. Setting Up Your Organization (Admin Only)
If you chose **Enterprise**, you are the "Global Admin." 

#### **Adding Team Members**
1.  Navigate to the **Admin Panel**.
2.  Click **Add User**.
3.  Choose between **Auto-generated Password** (convenient) or **Manual Password**.
4.  Assign a role: 
    *   **Employee**: Can only chat with approved SOPs.
    *   **Manager**: Can upload and manage SOPs.
    *   **Admin**: Full control over users and settings.

#### **Uploading SOPs**
*   Go to the **Knowledge Base**.
*   Upload your PDF documents.
*   Set **Access Levels** (e.g., restricted to Managers only or available to all Employees).

---

### 4. Chatting with your Knowledge Base
This is where the magic happens!
*   Type questions like: *"What is our remote work policy?"*
*   OpsMind will scan your uploaded PDFs and provide a cited answer.
*   **Sources**: Click on the source links to see exactly which page of the PDF the answer came from.
